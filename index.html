Our mission

helps socially phobic teens feel more comfortable and confident when socializing on social media.


  • AI-assistant
  • Find Friends by Interest
  • Cool “Like” interaction
  • Avoid comment violence

Whether browsing other people's posts or searching for new connections, our AI chat assistant is always on hand to provide helpful information, conversation starters, reassurance, assistance, and motivation to enhance the user experience and help users feel more comfortable and secure while using our app.

"Find Friends by Interests" is a feature designed to help socially phobic individuals connect with others who share similar interests. By selecting their hobbies and interests, users can be matched with like-minded individuals in a safe and comfortable environment. . With "Find Friends by Interests," socially phobic individuals can discover new connections and build meaningful relationships with others who understand and appreciate their passions.

The "Cool 'Like' interaction" feature is designed to create a positive and engaging social experience for users who struggle with social anxiety. Through the use of innovative design, such as incorporating unique and visually appealing ways for users to interact with each other's content, this feature aims to increase user confidence and promote positive social interactions.

Say goodbye to the fear of negative comments and hurtful interactions. Our "Avoid Comment Violence" feature utilizes advanced AI technology to detect and filter out toxic language, allowing you to engage with others in a safe and supportive environment. Connect with like-minded individuals and build meaningful relationships without the fear of judgment or criticism.

Design Thinking

Empathize

By understanding the struggles and concerns of socially phobic teens on social media, we can create an app that provides a safe and comfortable space for them to connect with others.

Through identifying key pain points, such as difficulty in initiating conversations and fear of rejection, we can design features that address these challenges and promote positive social interactions.

Define

Ideate

Brainstorming and generating creative solutions, such as the use of AI-generated chat options and a cool "like" interaction, can lead to innovative features that help users feel more confident and secure.

Through rigorous testing and user feedback, we can continuously iterate and improve the app to create a positive and effective experience for socially phobic teens on social media.
